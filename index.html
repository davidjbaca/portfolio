Hello there!

I'm a Austin-based software engineer with a background in Finance and the Real Estate industry. My work experience has always centered around people, their needs, their wants, and end goals. So I approach all my work the way I know best, with a clients' best intrest in mind.

Experience

In the Real Estate industry, you're in the business of laying your best foot forward, selling yourself and always showing the client that you care about them and their priorities just as much as they do. Anticipating needs, creating indulgence, and fulfilling happiness became second nature to me. I thought, what if I can do this on a larger scale?

Computer programming, when it comes down to it, is about people, about the human experience. In October 2022, I joined General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive because I wanted to contribute to that experience. Programming offers so much flexibility and creativity on how I can achieve that. I can help solve problems one application at a time to make the human experience more seamless, more efficient, and in my ideal world, more thoughtful.
