Hello there!

I'm an Austin, Texas-based software engineer with a background in Finance and Real Estate. My work experience has always centered around people their respective needed wants, and goals. Therefore, I approach all my work the way I know best, with a client's best interest in mind.

Experience

In the Real Estate industry, you're in the business of putting your best foot forward, selling yourself and always showing the client that you care about them and their priorities just as much as they do. Anticipating needs, creating indulgence, and fulfilling happiness became second nature to me. I thought, what if I can do this on a larger scale?

Computer programming, when it comes down to it, is about people, about the human experience. In October 2022, I joined General Assembly's Software Engineering certification program because I wanted to contribute to that experience. Programming offers so much flexibility and creativity on how I can achieve that. I can help solve problems one application at a time to make the human experience more seamless, more efficient, and in my ideal world, more thoughtful.

Portfolio

Here are some of my current and past projects.

ATM Works

Collaborative full stack project with a team of 3 engineers. ATM Works helps you track a ATM owners one or multiple locations they service, ATM's revenue, and current balance. Technologies: HTML, CSS, JS, Python, Django, Favicon, Mapbox API, Bootstrap.

Workout Buddy

Second project with General Assembly that puts you in a client's perspective of personal trainning. I decided to divide it into weeks to set your weekly goal of how many days the client can train, and they have the ability to make updates on a weekly basis to reflect their actual amount of training. Created with Express, MongDB, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and Google Auth.

Job Tracker(trouble with live site file too big for cyclic deployment)

Third solo project with in the General Assebly program. To aid those affected with the volatility in the job market, I created an application tracker, for all applications submitted and fields to update the status of each. Created with Mongoose, Express, React, Node.js, AWS S3 Bucket, Bcrypt, and Semantic Ui React.
